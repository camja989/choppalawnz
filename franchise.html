Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need lawn care experience?

No! We provide comprehensive training covering all technical and business aspects. Many of our most successful franchisees had no prior lawn care experience.

How much can I earn?

Average franchisees generate $100,000-$150,000 in annual revenue. After expenses and fees, take-home income typically ranges from $60,000-$90,000+ in the first year, growing significantly in subsequent years.

Can I run this part-time?

While it's possible to start part-time, full-time commitment yields the best results. Many franchisees start part-time and transition to full-time within 3-6 months as they build their customer base.

Are territories still available?

Yes! We're expanding throughout New Zealand. Popular areas include Auckland, Wellington, Christchurch, Hamilton, and Tauranga. Contact us to check availability in your preferred location.

What ongoing support do I receive?

You'll have a dedicated franchise manager, access to our 24/7 support line, monthly training webinars, annual conferences, and a private franchisee community forum for peer support and best practice sharing.
