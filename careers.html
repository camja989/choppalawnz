Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between employee and contractor positions?

Employees receive minimum wage with all equipment provided and are part of our close-knit team. Contractors use their own equipment, receive jobs via our app, and could earn $35+ per hour with flexible scheduling. Both are treated like family!

Do I need experience to apply?

Not at all! We provide comprehensive training for employee positions. Contractor positions work best if you have some experience and your own equipment, but we're happy to discuss options with motivated individuals.

What are the working hours?

We offer flexible scheduling with morning and afternoon shifts available. Most work is done Monday to Friday, with some Saturday opportunities for extra income.

Is this suitable for students?

Absolutely! Many of our team members are students who appreciate the flexible hours and good pay. We work around university schedules and exam periods.

What equipment do I need to provide?

Employees: Nothing! We provide all equipment, vehicles, and safety gear. Contractors: You'll need your own professional mowing equipment and reliable vehicle. We provide the app, customers, and support!
