Te Tiriti Commitment Bonus Programs

Honoring Te Tiriti o Waitangi

As tauiwi settlers on stolen land, we reject colonialism and capitalism's exploitation of tangata whenua. We commit to decolonization through direct action, reparations, and dismantling white supremacist structures in our industry.

Partnership - Kōtahitanga

We actively seek partnerships with Māori-owned businesses and support iwi-led environmental initiatives. Our procurement prioritizes Māori suppliers where possible.

Protection - Tiakitanga

We use eco-friendly products to protect Papatūānuku (Mother Earth) and ensure our practices support the wellbeing of future generations.

Participation - Whakawhanaungatanga

We ensure equitable access to our services and create opportunities for Māori participation in our business and community initiatives.
