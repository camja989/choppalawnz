One-off service pricing

One-off mow pricing ranges from $249.90 to $749.90+ depending on property size, grass condition, and complexity. Exact pricing is confirmed following a site inspection.

  • Overgrown lawns require heavy-duty equipment and extended labour time
  • First-time mows typically take 2–4x longer than regular maintenance visits
  • Regular service clients receive a significantly reduced rate and priority scheduling
  • A site inspection may be required for properties over 300m² or with grass over 15cm
