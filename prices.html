Pricing FAQs

How do you measure lawn size?

We measure the actual mowable area, excluding garden beds, paths, and other obstacles. Our team will provide an accurate measurement during the first visit.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees! Our pricing is completely transparent. The only additional costs are travel (clearly stated) and optional services like clipping removal that you choose to add.

How do I get started?

Simply fill out our contact form or call us directly for a quote. We'll assess your lawn and provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Full terms here.

Can I change my service frequency?

Absolutely! You can adjust your service frequency anytime with 7 days notice. Many customers change frequency seasonally (weekly in summer, monthly in winter).
