Between tenants? We handle it.

Property managers need outdoor maintenance done fast, done right, and documented properly. A missed inspection or an overgrown section at handover reflects poorly on you — not the tenant.

We specialise in end-of-tenancy yard tidies, section clearances, and ongoing maintenance for Wellington rental properties. You send us the address, we take care of the rest and send you photographic proof of completion.

What owners & managers ask for

  • Fast turnaround between tenants
  • No need to visit the site to check work
  • Clean before-and-after documentation
  • Professional, itemised invoices
  • A contractor who shows up on time
  • Reliable cover across your portfolio

What a full tidy-up includes

Every end-of-tenancy job is assessed on site. Below is what a typical handover visit covers — we’ll quote you based on the actual condition of the property.

  • Full lawn mow & line trimming
  • Crisp edging on paths and driveways
  • Weed removal from garden beds & paths
  • Leaf & debris clearance
  • Invasive growth removal (thistles, vines, gorse)
  • Overgrown section clearing
  • Green waste bagged & removed from site
  • Path & driveway sweep
