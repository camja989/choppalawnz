The Terrace — Multi-unit complex, Wellington CBD
Wellington CBD · Property management client

Full vegetation clearance and leaf removal at a multi-unit complex on The Terrace. The rear section had been left to overgrow significantly, creating an access issue and a safety hazard from accumulated leaf litter on the paths. We cleared all invasive growth, removed leaf debris from paths and steps, and hauled the green waste off-site. Photo completion report provided to the property manager on the same day.
