About Choppalawnz

We're passionate about creating beautiful outdoor spaces that enhance your lifestyle and bring joy to your everyday life.

Our Story

Choppalawnz was born from a simple idea: lawn care should be effortless, professional, and designed for modern busy lifestyles. Founded in Wellington, we recognized that people wanted more time to enjoy their beautiful outdoor spaces rather than maintaining them.

What started as a local service has grown into Wellington's most trusted lawn care company, serving hundreds of satisfied customers across the region. We've built our reputation on reliability, quality, and genuine care for both our customers and their gardens.
